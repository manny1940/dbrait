About

Travel and Tourism is the fastest growing industry in the world, with a potential that is limitless. It offers more employment opportunities than most other industries. The department offers value based quality education of International Standards. The department goal is to produce quality and well-groomed students for employment in hotel, tourism and services management positions at a national as well as global level. Students get to hone their skills and techniques on all fronts, with special emphasis on hotel operational and management skills. If you wish to build a career in the exciting hospitality and tourism industry, then build it with hotel management faculty of the Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Institute of Technology. The department was established in the month of July 1989. Initially the department offered Hotel Reception and Book Keeping (HRBK) and Housekeeping under the Board of Technical Education New Delhi. In the year of 2002, under the world bank project the department took initiative to start the diploma programme in Hotel Management courses. In the year of 2003 the Diploma Programme commenced with affiliation to Maharashtra Board of Technical Education (MSBTE).
