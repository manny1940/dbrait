Computer Science and Engineering

About

The Department of Computer Engineering was started in the year 1997 with the Post Diploma in Computer Applications Programme and subsequently Diploma in Computer Engineering was started in 1998. Seeing the demand and scope of Computer Application, Diploma in Information Technology has also been added in the year 2003. The department with the passage of its evolution and growth has succeeded in creating its place by producing quality products that are at par with their mainland counterparts. The Computer Science Engineering Department offers courses leading to Diploma in Computer Engineering, Diploma in Information Technology. The department has taken a leap with the commencement of B.Tech Programme in Computer Science & Engineering from the year 2009. All the programmes have been approved by AICTE. The Department of Computer Science & Engineering offers two Diploma Programs and one Degree Program. This department is also a resource center for Software development and Hardware maintenance for Andaman and Nicobar Administration. This Department offers three courses :

  1. B.Tech (Computer Science & Engineering)
  2. Diploma in Computer Engineering
  3. Diploma in Information Technology

Vision

To produce professionally competent and socially sensitive IT, Computer Engineers.

Mission

  • To achieve academic competence in core areas through structured teaching learning process.
  • To enhance employability skill of students by organizing workshops on emerging trends.
  • To enable our students to apply engineering skills in real time projects and to inculcate ethics by involving them in societal activities.
  • To provide a platform for developing leadership, management skills.

Lab info

  • CISCO / Networking Laboratory :

    This laboratory provides computing facility for the students of the department of Computer Engineering and Information Technology. It consists of more than 18 high end PCs running Microsoft Windows XP as well as Linux (Red Hat) with dual boot option on top of Intel Pentium IV processor & matching supporting hardware & peripherals and all wired together by the laboratory 100 mbps LAN. To top it, there are two dual processors Windows server running Windows 2000, with the latest add-ons, an Intel Xeona a generic Microsoft Windows NT version 4 servers, one on which is an NT terminal server powering the laboratory. Besides these, there is a local mirror server for Win NT and Win 2000 server to provide the most recent updates to the DBRAIT Port Blair community. Above all, future plan is to set up a high and server running Sun Solaris to be able for high end computational needs of the student's faculty of DBRAIT, Port Blair.

  • Computer Hardware Laboratory :

    The department computer hardware laboratory is equipped with educational tools to promote understanding of computer hardware and its peripherals among the students. 8085/86 Microprocessor Trainer kits and 8033 Microcontroller kits are used to provide hand on experience to learning students about basic hardware. These are ably supported by Colour Logic Analyzer and Function / Arbitrary wave generator, digital oscilloscopes, wireless transmitter /receiver pairs, Data Acquisition/Switch units, TDM pulse code modulator / transmitter and demodulator / receiver and other similar essentials. Students working on various projects have developed microcontroller controlled arms and robots and other similar hardware items.

  • Advanced Computer Laboratory :

    The department provides high end Pentium IV desktops to each of the final year DCE and IT students for their final year dissertation. The department allows the students to configure and maintain their PCs according to their wish and their needs. Additional support and peripherals are provided based on their requirement in the particular projects.

  • Basic Computer Laboratory :

    The regular DCE and IT students are provided with high end desktops machines which is amply supported by additional peripherals an hardware. Every student is made responsible for his / her equipment which is used for the project.

  • Software Testing Laboratory :
